Usage

Breadcrumb requires one or more p-menuitem elements. Menuitem has value, icon and href as available attributes. 
<p-breadcrumb>
    <p-menuitem href="#" value="Value of Item 1"></p-menuitem>
    <p-menuitem href="#" value="Value of Item 2"></p-menuitem>
</p-breadcrumb>
